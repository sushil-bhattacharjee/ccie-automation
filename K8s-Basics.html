4Secrets — Encoding vs. Encryption▶

Secrets use base64 encoding — NOT encryption. For encryption at rest: EncryptionConfiguration or HashiCorp Vault.

⚠️ echo "YWRtaW4=" | base64 -d → "admin". Not secure by default.

8Scaling — HPA, Manual, Formula▶
# Manual kubectl scale --replicas=4 deployment/my-app # Auto (default cpu-percent=80) kubectl autoscale deployment/my-app --min=2 --max=8 --cpu-percent=70 # Delete HPA kubectl delete hpa my-app

Formula: desiredReplicas = ceil[ currentReplicas × (currentMetric / desiredMetric) ]

--min = hard floor, --max = hard ceiling.
